- A girl is having recurring dreams and is confused about whether they are real or not
- She is attracted to a man named Xuzhu and they have a romantic encounter in a dark and cold place
- Xuzhu is hesitant to reveal the truth about their meeting and the girl believes it is all a dream
- They continue to meet for three days and their love grows, but Xuzhu is conflicted about his Buddhist beliefs
- The girl is eventually taken away by someone named Tonglao and Xuzhu is left longing for their time together
