- The monk named Yuan Gen is in charge of a vegetable garden and enjoys exerting his authority over other monks who are sent to work there as punishment.
- He interrogates a monk named Xu Zhu about his transgressions and accuses him of stealing and drinking alcohol.
- Xu Zhu admits to breaking the precepts and confesses to killing multiple people.
- Yuan Gen is initially shocked and afraid of Xu Zhu's violent tendencies, but then realizes that Xu Zhu's martial arts skills have been completely abolished.
- Yuan Gen is relieved and suggests that Xu Zhu wear shackles and handcuffs while working in the garden as per the monastery's rules for monks who have committed violent acts.
- Xu Zhu agrees to comply with the rules.
