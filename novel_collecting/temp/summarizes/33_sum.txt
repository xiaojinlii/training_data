- A man named Duan Yu encounters a statue made of white jade that resembles a woman
- The statue's eyes are made of black gemstones and seem to follow Duan Yu's movements
- Duan Yu addresses the statue as a "goddess" and expresses his admiration for her beauty
- Duan Yu notices inscriptions on the walls of the room, including quotes from the philosopher Zhuangzi
- Duan Yu speculates that the statue represents a woman named "Autumn Water" and that she is accompanied by a man named "Xiao Yaozi"
- Duan Yu reflects on the joy and happiness that the couple must have experienced in their secluded dwelling
